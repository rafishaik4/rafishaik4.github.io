Team Bring it!

Team Bring it was formed in 2005 with John Cena and The Rock then-heel rookies. The team would quickly win the WWE Tag Team Championships and hold onto them for 2 years(they lost then 3 times during the 2 years) and hold the longest multi-title regin in history. During this time the team would feud with The Hardy Boyz, Edge and Christian, The Brothers Of Destruction and Mankind and Hulk Hogan.
